Classes and times

Yoga

You have three different yoga classes to choose from. Vinyasa (athletic) on Mondays, Hatha (good for beginners) on Wednesdays and Yin (slow paced) on Saturdays.

Pilates

Our pilates classes come in three versions, so you can always find one you like. Classic (traditional) on Tuesdays, Mat (core) on Thursdays and Stott (balance) on Fridays.

Meditation

We have chosen to place the meditation classes at the end of the week, for less stress and better effect.

Private classes

You can always choose to book a private session with one of us. It can be a good start if you are new, or have not been practicing for a while.

About us

Chris

Chris has over 15 years of experience in yoga. He is a certified yoga teacher who has practiced with yogis in India and taught yoga in the US. Chris teaches Vinyasa, Hatha and Yin.

Liza

Liza is a certified pilates teacher who has travelled the world, learning from the best and teaching in both Australia and the UK. She teaches Classic, Mat and Stott.

Sarah

Sarah has spent many years learning different meditation techniques, and frequently visits schools and nursing homes to teach young and old how to make meditation part of their day.
